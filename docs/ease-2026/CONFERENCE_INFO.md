## Track Focus

Experience reports and perspective papers on empirical assessment in software engineering. Single-anonymous review (minimum three reviewers) evaluating originality, industrial relevance, significance, and generalizability.

### Topics of Interest

- Technology assessment and cost-benefit analysis
- Empirical prediction systems and estimation models
- Real-world technique evaluation
- Industrial experimentation (A/B testing, quasi-experiments)
- Product and process quality assessment
- Software engineering analytics and simulation studies
- Evidence-based approaches in industry contexts

## Submission Requirements

- **Format:** ACM Proceedings Format
- **Long papers:** 8 pages + 2 pages for references
- **Short papers:** 4 pages + 1 page for references
- **Language:** English
- **Content:** Original, unpublished work
